Data
- person
    - HAS: TFN, name, address
    - TYPES: authors, editors
- author
    - MAY HAVE: pen-name
- publisher
    - HAS: ABN, name, address
- book
    - HAS: title, edition number, isbn
Relationships
- Editors edit books
- Authors write books
Constraints
- Every person has exactly one distinct TFN
- Every editor works for exactly one publisher
- An editor cannot be an author
- Every book has exactly one editor
- each edition of book has one unique isbn
- each edition of a book is published by exactly one publisher

Assumptions
- This database only includes people who earn money
- Every book in the database is published
